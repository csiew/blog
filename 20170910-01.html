Merging this blog with my personal blog

10 September 2017

I’ve decided to eliminate the division between my personal blog and tech blog. As most of my life revolves around tech anyway, this wouldn’t be too much of a change from the original purpose of this blog.

Perhaps things I write or post from my personal life might help add some colour to this blog! I’ll consider picking up my childhood hobby of fictional story-writing again and posting these stories on this blog.
