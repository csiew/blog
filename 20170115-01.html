Hear Ye

15 January 2017

I’ve devised this little “digital town crier” using the News API and ResponseVoice.js JavaScript APIs. It grabs the top 10 headlines from a select few news sites, and reads them out-loud whilst giving the user visible, clickable links to those articles with their pictures above.

It’s still in development, but I guess this could be useful if you’re too lazy to go to each individual news site yourself or to load up a news aggregator app. I mean, it READS the headlines for you!
